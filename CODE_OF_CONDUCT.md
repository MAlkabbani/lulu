# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Lulu VAIA aims to be a respectful, constructive, and technically honest open-source project. We want contributors to feel safe asking questions, proposing improvements, and disagreeing about implementation details without personal attacks.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent, but discuss impact honestly
- keep feedback specific and actionable
- welcome contributors with different experience levels
- focus debate on code, docs, tests, and product direction

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, threats, or hateful language
- personal attacks or repeated bad-faith engagement
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- disruptive spam, trolling, or intentionally hostile review behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately using the guidance in `SECURITY.md` or through a maintainer contact channel you control.
